`Sent|Delivered|Read` status image and selects the message with `.last()`
(09-VERIFICATION WR-08 CONFIRMED BLOCKING RELEASE GAP). The community feed

competing visual noise against the design line (one primary action,
assigned-never-chosen, calm) and the settled community feed layout. Reload +
exact-count proves both that the send persisted through Supabase and that no
duplicate exists (optimistic + realtime + reload dedup).
