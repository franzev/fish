## Trust boundaries

| Boundary | Untrusted input crossing it | Required control |
|---|---|---|
| Signed delivery → memory/files | Short-lived URL/token and bytes | Memory-only lease, opaque key, displayed-only write, sentinel scan |
| Lifecycle/network → recovery | Bursty/unvalidated/data-saving signals | Coalescing, validated policy, attempts 0/1 |
| Raw auth → gallery identity | Missing/replaced/unresolved owner | Generation revoke and verified purge-before-bind |
