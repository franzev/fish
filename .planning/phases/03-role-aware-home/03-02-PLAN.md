| T-3-04 | Information Disclosure | coach-name read on client home | mitigate | The coach `display_name` read is scoped by Plan 01's `is_client_of` RLS policy (client reads only their own assigned coach's row); the page adds no service-role client, only the RLS-scoped anon/server client. |
