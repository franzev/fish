FISH · sketch 003 · profile
Design question

How much does a client see about themselves — without a scoreboard?

Progress already has its own tab, so the profile need not repeat it. Three framings of the client's own screen — identity-only, a reward-only reflection, or the coaching relationship out front. All monochrome, calm, reward-never-score. Tap Appearance to flip light / dark.

A · Essentials — you, your coach, your settings

The calmest read: who you are, who your coach is, and your preferences. No stats — progress lives in the Progress tab, so the profile stays a low-stakes, choice-free surface.

B · Reflection — a warm, reward-only look back

Adds a gentle “your progress” block: topics you’ve completed (never a score), what you’re working on, and how long you’ve been at it. Celebrates returning. Risk: it can start to duplicate the Progress tab and edge toward a scoreboard.
