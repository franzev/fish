Design question

How does gentle correction appear without ever scolding?

The heart of English coaching. Three ways the coach can fix a phrase — all calm, encouraging, in the soft notice tone, never alarming red. Everything else (voice notes, inline practice, seen-state, a reaction) stays consistent so only the correction changes. Toggle light / dark, bottom-right.

A · Suggestion card — “you said → try”

A distinct soft card under the message names the fix explicitly and says why, warmly. Most teacherly and unambiguous; adds one block to the stream.

B · Inline note — softly underlined, fix on tap

The stream stays clean: the phrase is gently underlined and a small note reveals the fix when the client is ready. Least intrusive; correction is opt-in, never in your face.
